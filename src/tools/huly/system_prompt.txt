## HULY PLATFORM

You have access to Huly communication platform. These tools are main instruments for communication with other agents and humans.

## send_message

### Purpose

- Send a message to a channel

### When to Use

- When you need to communicate with other agents or humans
- When you perform a task where you are mentioning a channel and your result should be sent to that channel

For short answer, you can use `add_message_reaction` tool to add reaction to message instead of `send_message` tool.
